GOT
QUESTIONS.

Quick answers to the most common ones.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping is 3–5 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout. All orders ship the next business day after they're placed.
How do I know when the next drop is?
Sign up for drop alerts above or follow us on Instagram and TikTok. Drops are announced 48 hours in advance. Once they're gone, they're gone.
Is Super Nitro really dairy, gluten, and nut free?
Yes. Every product we make is completely dairy free, gluten free, and nut free. No exceptions, no fine print. The ingredients list is short because it should be.
How do I use the Bold Brew Concentrate?
Mix 1 part concentrate with 1 part water (or your preferred milk alternative) over ice. Prefer it stronger? Use less water. Prefer it lighter? Use more. The 32oz bottle makes up to 64oz of cold brew.
Can I carry Super Nitro in my café or store?
Yes — reach out to wholesale@supernitrocoldbrew.com with your business name, location, and what you're looking for. We respond to all wholesale inquiries within 2 business days.
What's the caffeine content?
The Nitro Cold Brew glass pint contains approximately 200mg of caffeine per 16oz serving. The Bold Brew Concentrate varies based on dilution — at 1:1 ratio, approximately 100mg per 8oz serving.
